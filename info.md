## Project Evaluation Criteria
﻿
Projects that meet the minimum criteria will be scored by our team based on the following criteria: 
Originality: How unique and innovative are the project features? What new features have been added beyond those taught in the course?
Scope: How much of the course content does the project cover? How well are optional contents incorporated beyond the required materials?
Technical Quality: How clean is the code? Is it well-documented? Are tests properly included?
User Experience: How clear, intuitive, and enjoyable is the user experience of the project?
Readiness: How close is the project to being adopted by real users? Is it scalable and ready for practical use? Have all necessary practical features for real usage been included?
Potential Impact: What is the potential global impact of the project? Can it be applied to a wider audience?
